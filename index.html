How to Play

Challenge yourself as you race against the clock to type the randomly displayed words as quickly as you possibly can. For each accurate word that you type, a new word will appear and additional time will be added to the clock.

Accumulate points for each accurately typed word, but don't let the time run out or it'll be game over. It's a fun and exciting way to practice your typing skills that will have you hooked.

Want more of a challenge? Click on the settings icon on the bottom left to toggle between Easy, Normal, and Hard.
